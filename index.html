About the program

This program is designed to help Muslims from all backgrounds memorize the noble Qur'an with proper Tajweed and consistency. Whether you're a beginner or already on the journey, this flexible learning program supports you at every stage.

Who is it for?

The Program is open to:


  • Beginners who have never memorized before
  • Those who want to revise or continue their hifdh
  • Students who need a structured and consistent routine
  • Adults, teens, and even children(with guidance)

    • Learning Format

    • Online via Whatsapp
    • Flexible Timing
    • Daily or Weekly Targets (Based on your pace and level)
    • Support Group and Motivation

    What You'll Learn

    • Memorization of Qur'an (Hifdh): You'll memorize verses step by step, following a consistent plan tailored to your level
    • Proper Tajwid & Pronounciation: Learn how to recite the Qur'an correctly with clear accurate Arabic pronounciation.
    • Revision strategies: You'll learn how to review and retain what you've memorized so it's stays with you long-term
